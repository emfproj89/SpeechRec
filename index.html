Listener

The Listener is a speech-to-text program. It attempts to recognize your speech and type it for you. Please keep in mind that the Listener is not spot-on. Listener is only supported on Chrome browser on Windows PC.

Speaker

The Speaker is a text-to-speech program. To use the Speaker, you type in the phrase, then click on the microphone image, and it will read aloud for you.
